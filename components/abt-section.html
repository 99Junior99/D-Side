The D-Side
ORIGIN

The origin of D-Side In the beginning of the 90s, at a cramped garage in the suburbs of Detroit, four friends decided to turn their frustration and passion for music into something great. This is how D-Side was born, a rock band that mixed heavy riffs, introspective lyrics and an electrifying stage presence.

THE CREW

The Vocalist, Jack "Howl" Carter, a former factory worker with a husky, soulful voice, found in the skillful guitarist, Danny Vance, a perfect partner to create striking melodies.
The band's kitchen was formed by bassist Mikey "Bones" Reynolds, whose energetic touch gave the right groove, and explosive drummer Tyler Knox, known for his frenetic style.

the history

After years of playing in bars and small concert halls, D-Side got their big break in 1997, when a home-recorded demo tape found its way into the hands of an influential producer. Their debut album, From Inside Out, launched the band to stardom with hits like "Slide" and "Shadows in the Rain."
